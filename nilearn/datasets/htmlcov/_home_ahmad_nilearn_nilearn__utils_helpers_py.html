83def _transfer_deprecated_param_vals(replacement_params, kwargs): 

84 """For the decorator replace_parameters(), reassigns new parameters 

85 the values passed to their corresponding deprecated parameters. 

86 

87 Parameters

89 replacement_params : Dict[str, str] 

90 Dictionary of old_parameters as keys with replacement parameters 

91 as their corresponding values.

95 

96 Returns 

97 ------- 

98 kwargs : Dict[str, any] 

99 Dictionary of all the keyword args to be passed on 

100 to the decorated function, with old parameter names

102

104 for old_param, new_param in replacement_params.items():
